REGIONAL CONCERT ASSOCIATION RECIPROCITY LIST – 2016-2017 SEASON

 

One of the distinct added-value features of being a Lompoc Concert Association season ticket holder is a privilege called reciprocity. This means that your season ticket provides the opportunity to attend the concerts of other participating associations at no extra charge. Our reciprocity network includes 15 other associations.





Offering reciprocity is a courtesy extended by each association and based on ability to seat visitors.  If seating is available, visitors may be asked to present a current subscription/membership card and/or wait until local subscribers are seated.   Please contact the association to confirm availability, as well as venue location and directions.
