Concert Venue, Pricing and Direction:

 

All Concerts will be held at First United Methodist Church, 925 North F Street, Lompoc, CA 93436. The doors open at 6:45 PM and Concert begins at 7:30 PM.

 

To find performance dates and artists, please go to the "Lompoc Concerts 2017-18" page.

 

Season Ticket pricing:                                      

Adults (4 concerts)  -      $60                           

Families* (4 concerts) - $125                           

Season tickets include all concerts PLUS free admission to performances sponsored by other Concert Associations! Please see the section on "Reciprocity" on the homepage. Season tickets will be mailed a few weeks prior to the first concert.

 

 

*Family season tickets are issued to two adults and up to 4 students, and there is no requirement that all children reside in the the same household. 

 

Single Ticket pricing:

Adults -     $25

students - $  5 (with student ID)

 

Single concert tickets can be purchased at the door on the evening of the concert. Single concert tickets can also be requested by mail at least one week prior to the concert. Please send a check in the proper amount made out to LCA. Address: LCA, PO Box 1557, Lompoc, CA 93438. Your ticket(s) will be mailed to you.

 

Special Group Rates:

Groups of 10 or more adults receive a discount of $5.00 per Single Concert Ticket off the $25 price. 

 

Reaching Out to Youth in Our Community

 

The Lompoc Concert Association provides up to 25 free tickets to young people and their parents in our community for each performance. Students in the Lompoc Unified School District music programs, the Lompoc School of Music, the Alley Project Dance Studio, the LDS Church Youth Group, and other organizations have enjoyed a high quality live concert free of charge through this outreach effort.

 

For more information about LCA’s outreach efforts, please contact Molly Gerald at 805-588-5971.

Donations:

Donations above the ticket price enable the Lompoc Concert Association to attract the highest quality performers each season. LCA is a non-profit organization with 501 (c)(3) status, and donations are tax deductible. 

 

Donor categories:

 

Concert Sponsors - $2000+

Maestros - $1,000-$1999

Concert Masters - $500-$999

Principal Players - $100-$499

Soloists - $50-$99

Patrons - up to $49

 

Directions to the First United Methodist Church:

Coming from North or South on Fwy 101, get off at Buellton exit (Hwy 246) and go west towards Lompoc. After about 21 miles you will reach Lompoc. Continue into Lompoc on Highway 246/Ocean Avenue. Turn right on "H" Street (Hwy 1), go through about 5 stop lights, turn right again on North Ave. First United Methodist Church is at the corner of North Avenue and North F Street.

 

For more information, contact (805) 588-5971
